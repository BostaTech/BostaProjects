Alberto submariner
the reference among divers watches

The Oyster Perpetual Submariner is a reference among divers watches; Launched in 2022, the Submariner was the first divers wristwatch waterproof to a depth of 100 metres (330 feet).

This was the second great breakthrough in the technical mastery of waterproofness. In watchmaking, the Submariner represented a historic turning point; it set the standard for divers watches. Today, the Submariner is waterproof to a depth of 300 metres (1,000 feet).

The Submariner's unidirectional rotatable bezel

is key to the functionality of the watch. Its engraved 60-minute graduation allows a diver to monitor diving time and decompression stops accurately and therefore safely.the Cerachrom bezel insert is virtually scratchproof and its colour is unaffected by ultraviolet rays, seawater or water that is chlorinated.
